About Us

  1. Home

Jefferson Park Veterinary Clinic is a full service, high quality, small animal veterinary practice.

When it comes to health care, we know you want the best for your feline or canine companions. Our team of caring, professional staff, our doctors and consulting specialists (radiologists and surgeons) strives to provide the best possible veterinary care for your pet.

Our facility is large enough to provide the latest medical treatments and specialty diagnostics, but small enough to offer you and your pet personal, caring individualized care. . The clinic has an open concept and is bathed in sunlight from skylights and many windows.

We have a state of the art clinical laboratory, anesthesia and patient monitoring equipment, a surgical suite, radiology, ultrasound, EKG, endoscope, oxygen cage and heated cages for post-op recovery.

Dr. Mandell and our caring professional staff are dedicated to the well-being of your pet. We look forward to working with you to keep your pet healthy and happy, now and for years to come.
