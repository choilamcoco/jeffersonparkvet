    Location

    Welcome to Jefferson Park Veterinary Clinic in West Sacramento!

    Our clinic has been serving the West Sacramento since October of 2011 for 9 years. We aspire to provide a full range of affordable, caring, and compassionate, high quality veterinary services for your canine and feline companions.

    Dr. Mandell and our caring professional staff are dedicated to the well-being of your pet. We look forward to working with you to keep your pet healthy and happy, now and for years to come.

    Our consultants, board certified veterinary radiologists and veterinary surgeons, cardiologist consult at our practice to provide their expert services on site.

    Frequently Asked Questions

    • Are we accepting new clients during the pandemic?

      Yes. We are accepting new clients right now, however, our appointments are usually booked up for few weeks. Please give us a call to schedule an appointment, and we will try our best to fit you into our schedule or cancellation list. If you have an emergency, please contact our nearby emergency hospitals (information in Pet Care Resources). If you have made an appointment with us, please fill out the new client information form prior to your visit. New Client Information

    • What are the clinic protocols for COVID-19?

      Our clinic follows strict COVID-19 protocols suggested by CDC. We are only allowing curbside drop-off appointments and only patients are allowed to enter our facility. We are sorry for the inconvenience, and please check out our COVID-19 protocols for more information. COVID-19 Update

    • Am I allowed to come in to the clinic with my pet?

      Unfortunately, due to current COVID-19 situation, all clients are not allowed to come in to our facility. We will take great care of your pets when they were dropped off for appointment.

    • My pet has an emergency, can I come in as a same day appointment?

      We usually have one or two same day appointment slots available every morning for emergency appointments in first-come-first-serve basis. However, there is no guarantee that we will be able to fit your emergency to our same day given our packed schedule. We will try our very best. If we are not able to fit you in, we reccommend contacting Vista Veterinary Specialist or nearby emergency clinics for emergency. For more information please visit our emergency pet care page.
